Shades of Green

The greatest inspiration for shades of green comes from nature, as evidenced by the names given to many of these colors. Some of the biologically-inspired shades are lime, mint, moss, avocado, and forest green. Other notable green shades are emerald, army green, olive green, and Kelly green.
